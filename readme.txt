== Description ==

Lite Google Map is a lite weight maps plugins. 

Major features in Lite Google Map include:

* Shortcode: [google_maps] use this shortcode on your post,page or widget.
* Maps Key: Add your google maps key.
* Latitude: Add your location Latitude.
* Longitude: Add your location Longitude.
* Zoom: zoom in Out your map.
* Maps Height: Increase/decrees your map height.
* Map Icon: Change your default Map Marker.
* Map's Theme: Change your default theme.


PS: You'll need an [API key](https://developers.google.com/maps/documentation/javascript/get-api-key) to use it. Ensure that Internet connection is available when used by localhost.

== Installation ==

Upload the Google Map Lite plugin to your blog, Activate it, then go to the admin menu -> Google Map Settings & setup your map with your configuration. Copy shortcode [google_maps] & paste on your post, page, widget. 

You're done! :)
